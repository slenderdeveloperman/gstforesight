Methodology
Predictions are generated by a multi-signal probabilistic engine that reads from the full CBIC circular corpus, GST Council minutes, ICAI representations, budget speeches, and High Court / Supreme Court judgments. Signals include: deferred agenda items, multi-forum judicial splits, ICAI representation frequency, and budget speech action language. Each signal is weighted and combined into a single probability estimate.
Outcome verification
Outcomes are manually verified against official CBIC circulars, GST Council press releases, and PIB notifications. A prediction is scored as a "hit" if the event occurred within the stated horizon window. A "miss" is recorded if the window expired with no corresponding regulatory action. Partial outcomes (advisory committee recommendation without final circular) are not scored until complete.
